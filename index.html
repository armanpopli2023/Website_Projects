Brief Bio

I'm a cloud and AI infrastructure engineer focused on deploying language models in high-compliance environments. My work spans LLM ops, RAG pipelines, agentic workflows, evaluation frameworks, and cloud-native infrastructure — with experience across modern cloud tools and model testing at scale.

I started on the testing side — reading model outputs and policy documents side by side, building early frameworks to evaluate when a model was right, wrong, or misleading. That experience shaped how I approach both systems and trust: not just as technical problems, but as interpretive ones.

I see engineering as more than implementation. I enjoy working on teams where there's space to think critically, ask hard questions, and stay curious — especially when the system is complex, high-stakes, or hard to define. I tend to focus on the logic behind the system — how it works, why it fails, and what we're really optimizing for.

That's why I'm especially drawn to the edge where AI meets institutional behavior: how systems earn trust, distribute power, and adapt quickly enough to meet the needs of a fast-changing society.
